For Plotinus love is the freely created gift of the One or the Good (Ennead VI 8 15; VI 7 22) and acts as an incitement for the soul to love and return to him to experience absolute simplicity or unity (Hadot 1988 p.59). (pg 64)

3.3 The Generation of Intellect as Being and Beings Continuing the process from the previous section will demonstrate how Plotinus followed the Pythagoreans who considered musical harmonics to be the a priori structure for the generation of and order in the universe (pg 75 )

Intellect is ontologically and epistemologically unified simultaneously as subject and object knower and known i.e. it knows itself as content and thinker of its content and yet its thoughts are determined by the One (Emilsson 2007 pp. 4-5). (pg 76)

The fractalisation of Intellect is consistent with the mechanism of harmonic generation (pg 80)

Plotinus describes Intellect as the principle of necessity ( Ennead V.3.6 10-11) operating as a “law of being” by which Beings are generated ( Ennead V.9.5 26-29). (pg 80)
